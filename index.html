AI Reimbursement

Intelligent Expense Management & Reimbursement App

About the App

AI Reimbursement is a comprehensive expense management application designed to streamline the process of capturing, analyzing, and processing business expenses. The app leverages artificial intelligence to automatically extract and categorize expense data from receipt images, making expense reporting effortless and accurate.

Key Features:

  • AI-powered receipt scanning and data extraction
  • Automatic expense categorization
  • Per diem (VMA) calculation for business trips
  • Mileage tracking and reimbursement calculation
  • Multi-language support (English & Chinese)
  • Secure cloud storage and data synchronization
  • Comprehensive trip management
  • Real-time expense reporting and analytics

How It Works

Simply create a business trip, capture photos of your receipts, and let our AI technology handle the rest. The app automatically extracts relevant information, calculates reimbursements, and generates comprehensive expense reports. Perfect for business travelers and organizations looking to modernize their expense management process.
